    What does this do?

    The deeritor is essentially a way to make deer (though not exclusively) through a web interface, for easy porting to IRC.

    The deer is safely saved with the deerkins bot, which used to reside in #satf and #/a/ on irc.rizon.net.
    When you save, you will be prompted to choose a name for your creation, if the name is taken, it will automatically random a short string as a suffix.
    You will then be returned your creation in kinskode and IRC codes.
    kinskode is a shorthand for IRC codes that sucks - but it's easier for this page and deerkins to interpret this sort of code. Albeit for lazy reasons.
    To output your deer, type deerme <deer> in a channel where deerkins is, and deerkins will start to spew out the creation, alternatively you can copy the output from mIRC/IRC codes and paste yourself.

    The regexes for converting text to canvas is only working in Firefox currently, and I'm not too keen on shizzling with regex/encoding to get other browsers working too, yet.
